About Us

The Every Crumb Counts Initative is a non-profit media outlet sharing resources about hunger awareness in Peel.

On this website, you can find a variety of information about hunger in Peel. This website serves as an outlet bringing you the latest news on hunger in the local community. We will share our thoughts on the issue through our weekly podcast as well as our own blog posts about certain topics and organizations. This is a one-stop learning point where you can read, listen, and watch information about hunger be presented to you. Learn the severity of the problem, where to donate, and what else you can do to help.

Learn about businesses and organizations you should support

Listen to our podcast or read our articles to learn about local businesses that are doing their part to combat hunger in Peel.

Engage with our media through videos, articles, and podcasts.

You can either listen, watch, or hear our information. There is something for everybody at Every Crumb Counts.

Understand the statistics behind hunger in Peel

World Hunger is a problem that is heavily talked about. However, we need to learn more about what happens in our local community. This is the platform for that.

Weekly Content all for Free

At Every Crumb Counts, all of our content is for free. We update content weekly by adding new podcast episodes, blog posts, and external videos and articles to our page. We want to make sure everyone has access to the website and can easily learn more about Hunger in Peel.

By listening to our podcast, you help us gain revenue to donate to local food banks. All of your support is appreciated. This is a 6 month initiative that will last until Janruary 2021. After then, all of our content will remain on our site for future generations to read and learn.

Why do we do it?

Every Crumb Counts is a 6-month project led by High School Students. We want to share resources and research on hunger in Peel and share them freely on a platform that anyone can access.

This is our first time learning how to manage a podcast and news outlet, and we are thrilled you are joining us by simply visiting our website.

Hunger is a prevalent issue in the world. However, with so much going on, many people become blinded and believe that it isn't a issue in Peel. The truth is, Hunger isn't a topic that isn't so distant. It can be an issue affecting someone we know and love. This local source will provide you with everything you need to know.
